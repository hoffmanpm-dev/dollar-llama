Why I Built This

Every investor I know started with a spreadsheet they built from scratch. Most of those spreadsheets had errors. The commercial tools cost $50 to $200 a year and force you to choose one strategy before running numbers. I wanted something better: a free tool that compares all three approaches at once, runs entirely in your browser, and doesn't require an account.

This calculator uses the same financial formulas I use in my day job — PMT-based amortization, Newton's method IRR, and time-value-of-money projections. Your inputs are saved locally on your device and never sent to a server. Read the full story →
